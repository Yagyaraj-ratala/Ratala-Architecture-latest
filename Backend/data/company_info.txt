COMPANY OVERVIEW
============================================================
Ratala Architecture and Interiors is a creative architecture and interior design agency based in Kathmandu, Nepal. The firm specializes in innovative, functional, and high-quality architectural and interior design solutions for residential and commercial projects. The company delivers integrated design and construction services, ensuring that projects are executed seamlessly from concept development to final completion.

Ratala Architecture and Interiors focuses on creating inspiring, practical, and sustainable built spaces that enhance daily living and business operations. The firm emphasizes professional design execution, technical accuracy, and long-term value in every project it undertakes.

INDUSTRY EXPERIENCE
============================================================
Ratala Architecture and Interiors has more than 12 years of professional experience in architecture, interior design, and construction planning. The firm initially operated in Bangalore, India, where it gained hands-on exposure to modern architectural practices, advanced construction techniques, and smart-home technologies.

After establishing a strong professional foundation in Bangalore, the company expanded its operations to Nepal. This transition was driven by a mission to contribute to national development through innovative, sustainable, and technically sound architectural solutions.

CORE PHILOSOPHY AND VALUES
============================================================
The core philosophy of Ratala Architecture and Interiors is centered on delivering 100 percent quality in every project. The firm believes that architecture and interior design should prioritize durability, functionality, and long-term performance rather than focusing solely on visual aesthetics.

All projects are guided by experienced professionals to ensure that design intent, execution quality, and client expectations are consistently aligned. The company values transparency, professional ethics, and accountability throughout the project lifecycle.

============================================================
DESIGN APPROACH
============================================================
Ratala Architecture and Interiors follows a realistic, pragmatic, and context-driven design approach. Each project is carefully analyzed based on multiple influencing factors to ensure practicality, compliance, and cultural relevance.

Key design considerations include:
- Vaastu principles and spatial orientation
- Site context and environmental conditions
- Cultural and historical background
- Local building codes and planning regulations
- Sustainability and environmental responsibility

This approach ensures that all designs are functional, efficient, culturally appropriate, and environmentally responsive.
